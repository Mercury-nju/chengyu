- `privacy.html` - Privacy Policy (App Store compliant)
- `terms.html` - Terms of Service (App Store compliant)

## Compliance

The Privacy Policy and Terms of Service have been updated to meet:

✅ **App Store Review Guidelines**
- Developer contact information included
- Clear data collection and usage policies
- Subscription terms and refund policy
- Children's privacy (COPPA compliance)
- Medical disclaimer for wellness apps

✅ **GDPR Compliance** (for EU users)
- Legal basis for processing
- User rights clearly stated
- Data retention policies

✅ **CCPA Compliance** (for California users)
- California privacy rights section
- No sale of personal information

✅ **Apple-Specific Requirements**
- Sign in with Apple terms
- In-App Purchase terms
- Apple third-party beneficiary clause

## Key Features

- No Screen Time data collection
- All data stored locally on device
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- Clear subscription and refund policies
- Comprehensive medical disclaimer

## Local Preview

To preview the website locally:

```bash
cd website
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

Then open http://localhost:8000 in your browser.
