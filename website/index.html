The Problem We See

In this age of information overload, our attention is constantly fought over by countless apps. Short videos, social media, instant messaging... they're meticulously designed to be irresistible.

We find ourselves increasingly unable to focus, increasingly anxious. Our brains are exhausted from high dopamine stimulation, and our psychological stability gradually crumbles.

02

Our Thinking

The problem isn't technology itself, but that we've lost connection with our inner selves.

We need an "anchor," a tool that can help us return to the present and rebuild psychological stability. Not to escape the digital world, but to find balance within it.
