The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app functionality

  • Processing subscriptions and paid features

4. Information Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your practice data, voice recordings, settings, and records are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. We do not upload this data to cloud servers or external databases.

Data Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Data encryption using iOS built-in encryption
  • Secure authentication through Sign in with Apple
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers
  • Access control to prevent unauthorized access

Data Location: Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data remains in your physical location and under your control.
