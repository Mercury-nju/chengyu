Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Welcome to Lumea (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the App"). We take your privacy protection and personal information security very seriously. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we collect your Apple ID associated information (user identifier), but we do not obtain your password.

Usage Data:

  • Meditation practice records (duration, frequency)
  • Mindfulness practice completion status
  • Stability Value and Cognitive Load Index
  • App usage preferences

Screen Time Data: With your explicit authorization, we access your device's Screen Time data to calculate the Cognitive Load Index. This data is processed locally only and is not uploaded to servers.

1.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, phone number, email address, or other personal identification information
  • We do not collect your location information
  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, or other sensitive information
  • We do not track your behavior in other apps or websites

2. Information Use

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Calculating and displaying your Stability Value, Cognitive Load Index, and other personal data
  • Saving your practice records and preferences
  • Processing subscriptions and paid features
  • Providing customer support

3. Information Storage

Local Storage: All your practice data, settings, and records are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to cloud servers.

Data Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including data encryption and access control.

4. Information Sharing

We commit to:

  • Not selling your personal information
  • Not sharing your personal information with third parties, except when:
    • We have your explicit consent
    • Required by law or regulation
    • Protecting our legitimate interests

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

Sign in with Apple: Used for account authentication, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Store In-App Purchase: Used for processing subscription payments, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Withdraw authorization (such as Screen Time access permission)

8. Data Retention

Your data will remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the data. After deleting the app, all local data will be permanently deleted.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published in the app and on the website, with the update date noted. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications.
